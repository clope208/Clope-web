My name is Cindy Lopez a 26 year old from Huntington Beach, California. I enjoy the ocean, running, and spending my time with my dog River.

My professional career first began as a photographer. Immediately after high school I went to the Art Institute of California - Orange County to pursue an associates of science degree in digital photography where I gained a large understanding, networked, and obtained many experiences for my profession as a photographer. When I finished, I was a freelance photographer. I worked on many projects such as for Orange Coast Magazine, Marketing companies, and also wedding and family photography. On Sundays for many years, I dedicated my days to photographing newborn babies at St. Joseph Hospital. This experiences exposed me to the medical profession in which I was inspired to pursue a different career helping children.

With the inconsistency of photography and my yearn to make a difference in our society, I decided to go back to school, Orange Coast College, to pursue a different career. I went to school and decided I wanted to pursue a degree in Psychology with an emphasis on the brain. After finishing community college, I transferred to the University of Riverside where I'm currently exploring Neuroscience and Psychobiology. As of now, my professional career is still being written in which I looked forward to seeing how it all unravels.
